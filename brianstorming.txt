The Emergency App
Sometime people do not have enough time to call the ambulance, police, disaster management or other services when they need it urgently.
My app will help dial all services urgently needed especially in this pandemic. Using my app the services are just 2 clicks away without logging in. 


What does my app does?
My app helps by calling the services as soon as possible. This app is only for a few cities as of now but in further updates it will be for people all over India
